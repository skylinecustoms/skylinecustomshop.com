Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of window tint film do you use?

We use Pro Nano ceramic window film exclusively. Unlike cheaper dyed films that fade to purple and bubble within 2-3 years, ceramic film blocks up to 60% more heat, provides 99% UV protection, and comes with a lifetime warranty against bubbling, peeling, and fading.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our 5-year ceramic coating system lasts 5+ years with proper maintenance (regular washing, no automatic car washes). The multi-layer application and paint correction ensure maximum durability.

What is paint correction and why does it matter?

Paint correction removes swirls, scratches, and oxidation through clay bar decontamination, iron removal, and multi-stage polishing. This is critical—if you seal scratches under ceramic coating, you're locking in damage forever. We always correct paint before coating.

Do you offer same-day service?

Sometimes, depending on our schedule. Most days we can accommodate same-day tinting. Ceramic coating requires 1-2 days. Contact us to check availability.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. We work by appointment only to ensure each vehicle gets the time and attention it deserves. Book online or text us to schedule.

Do you work on all vehicle types?

Yes—sedans, coupes, trucks, SUVs, luxury vehicles, Teslas, sports cars—we handle them all. Our climate-controlled facility is equipped for any vehicle type.
